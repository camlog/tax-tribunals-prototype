Do you want appeal now?

Your appeal to the tax tribunal will cost £20

What you'll need

  • your name and address (and your representative’s if you have one)
  • copy of the review decision letter from HMRC to upload (e.g. a scan or PDF)
  • details of your dispute, why you’re appealing and the result you want
  • credit or debit card to pay your appeal fee

    • What are fees for?

    • your fees are paid to HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS) towards the cost of an independent judgement of your case
    • you may have to pay further fees depending on your case and whether it needs a hearing
    • all tax tribunal fees will be refunded by HMRC (or other government agency) if your appeal is successful.
    • >if you’re on a low income or receiving certain benefits, you may be able to get help with fees

      • What happens next

      The tribunal will process your appeal and get back to you within 30 days. You’ll either receive an immediate decision on your case or details of a hearing, which you may have to attend in person.

      You’ll also receive an invoice for your payment of 20 to start your tax tribunal appeal.
